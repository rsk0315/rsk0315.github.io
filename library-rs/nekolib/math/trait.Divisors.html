約数列挙。

Complexity

$O(\sqrt{n})$ time, $O(1)$ space.

Examples

use nekolib::math::Divisors;

let div: Vec<_> = 60_u64.divisors().collect();
assert_eq!(div, [1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 10, 12, 15, 20, 30, 60]);
